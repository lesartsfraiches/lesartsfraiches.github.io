The Gothic Quarter I


The Gothic Quarter in Barcelona is the center of the old city. It stretches from La Rambla to Via Laietana, and from the Mediterranean seafront to Ronda de Sant Pere. It is a part of Ciutat Vella district. In Catalan it's called Barri Gòtic, in Spanish it's Barrio Gótico. While walking through the narrow streets at night I found this wonderfully decorated lamppost. The play of shadows and light makes it look almost like a painting.
