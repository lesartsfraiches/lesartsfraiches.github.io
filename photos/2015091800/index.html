The Gothic Quarter III


The Gothic Quarter in Barcelona is the center of the old city. It stretches from La Rambla to Via Laietana, and from the Mediterranean seafront to Ronda de Sant Pere. It is a part of Ciutat Vella district. In Catalan it's called Barri Gòtic, in Spanish it's Barrio Gótico. The photography captures perfectly the macabre and dark mystique of the Gothic era in Europe.

The photography is printed without the watermark, carefully packaged and delivered worldwide.
